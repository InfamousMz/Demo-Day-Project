New to the Gaming Community

HELLO FUTURE GAMERS!!! Welcome to the world known as Esports a place to have fun with others who crave the adrenaline of playing their favorite games. Or if you're seeking popularity and don't know how to don't worry we'll help get you through to the tons of tournaments to get your name known by other fellow gamers or espports teams looking for new recruits if you show that you have what it takes to play competively. Esports will allow you to create bonds/friendships with other gamers, and if you choose you have the ability to form teams to stay connected with those people both inside and outside the gaming community. Take it a step further to compete against other teams when notified of any team tournaments for fun or if your team is in it for the prize and recognition as a formidable team.

How to Get Started?

Now future gamers lets start your journing in the world of Esports. If you may head to our "Socials" page to link one of your socials to get notified of any future social gatherings or future tournaments. Wheever you're ready head to the "Where to" page to find local gaming lounges or cafes to connect with other fellow gamers in person. Just make sure to use caution but always have fun.
